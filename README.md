# Social Media Engagement Dashboard
![Social Media Engagement Dashboard](./Social_Media_Engagement_Dashboard.png)

## Overview

The Social Media Engagement Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze social media performance across multiple platforms, campaigns, and content formats.

The dashboard enables marketing teams to monitor audience engagement, evaluate campaign effectiveness, identify high-performing content, and make data-driven decisions to improve overall digital marketing performance.

---

## Business Objective

The primary objective of this project is to:

- Track social media marketing performance using key engagement metrics.
- Measure audience reach and interaction across platforms.
- Evaluate campaign effectiveness and content performance.
- Identify high-performing content formats and themes.
- Support data-driven marketing decision-making.

## Dashboard Features

### Platform Performance Analysis
Compares impressions generated across social media platforms to identify the highest-performing channels.

### Post Type Engagement Analysis
Evaluates engagement rates across different content formats including Carousel, Video, Image, Reel, and Text posts.

### Content Theme Performance
Analyzes audience interaction across various content themes to identify preferred content categories.

### Campaign Reach Distribution
Visualizes campaign contribution to total audience reach and highlights campaign effectiveness.

### Engagement Trend Analysis
Tracks engagement performance over time and helps identify patterns and fluctuations.

## Key Insights

- Generated over **3 Million Impressions** and **2 Million Reach** across social media campaigns.
- Achieved an average engagement rate of approximately **15.7%**.
- Instagram emerged as the highest-performing platform in terms of impressions.
- Carousel posts generated the highest engagement rate among all content formats.
- Promotional and Educational content themes received stronger audience interaction.
- Campaign performance varied significantly, highlighting opportunities for optimization.
- Engagement trends fluctuated over time, indicating the importance of content planning and audience targeting.

---

## Recommendations

- Prioritize investment in high-performing platforms.
- Increase the use of carousel-based content formats.
- Focus on educational and promotional content themes.
- Optimize lower-performing campaigns through improved targeting and messaging.
- Conduct regular A/B testing to improve content effectiveness.
- Monitor KPIs continuously to support data-driven marketing decisions.
- Strengthen audience engagement through interactive and value-driven content.
